About Ian
Actor
Ian Josep Somerhalder is an American actor, model,
activitist and director. He is known for playing Boone Carlyle
in the TV drama Lost, Damon Salvatore in the CW's
supernatural drama The Vampire Diaries and Dr. Luther
Swann in Netflix's sci-fi horror serires V Wars.
